Our Detailing Services

Discover the range of professional detailing services we offer to make your vehicle shine like new, inside and out.

Exterior Detailing

Restore your vehicle's shine with our comprehensive exterior wash, wax, and paint protection services.

Our exterior detailing process includes:

  • Thorough hand wash and dry
  • Clay bar treatment to remove contaminants
  • High-quality wax application for gloss and protection
  • Paint sealant for long-lasting defense
  • Tire and rim cleaning and dressing
  • Window cleaning (exterior)

We ensure every inch of your car's exterior is meticulously cleaned and protected, bringing back that showroom finish.

Interior Cleaning

Experience a spotless and fresh interior with our deep cleaning, vacuuming, and surface conditioning.

Our interior cleaning package features:

  • Complete vacuuming of carpets, seats, and trunk
  • Shampooing of upholstery and carpets (if applicable)
  • Leather cleaning and conditioning
  • Dashboard, console, and door panel cleaning and protection
  • Window cleaning (interior)
  • Odor elimination

We pay attention to every detail, ensuring your car's interior is not just clean, but also a healthy and comfortable environment.

Wheel & Tire Care

Specialized cleaning and protection for your wheels and tires, removing brake dust and adding shine.

Our wheel and tire care includes:

  • Deep cleaning of rims to remove brake dust and grime
  • Tire scrubbing and cleaning
  • Application of tire dressing for a lasting shine and protection
  • Wheel well cleaning
  • Optional rim polishing and sealing

Clean wheels and tires make a huge difference in your car's overall appearance. We make them look their best.

Full Package

The ultimate detailing experience, combining our exterior and interior services for a complete vehicle transformation.

Our full package is the best value and includes everything from our Exterior Detailing and Interior Cleaning services, plus:

  • Engine bay cleaning (optional, upon request)
  • Headlight restoration (if needed)
  • Comprehensive attention to every detail

Choose the full package for a vehicle that looks and feels brand new, inside and out. It's the perfect solution for preparing a car for sale or simply treating yourself and your ride.
